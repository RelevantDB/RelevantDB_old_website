Careers at RelevantDB

Senior software developer
Chicago, Illinois
Onsite or remote

We are developing a particular type of NewSQL database designed to run on the JVM.

We're looking for people with several years of software development experience, particularly around creating database engines or search engines. We're looking for experience with at least some of the following: distributed systems, compilers and parser-generators, internal database design, various index types and strategies, compression, kv stores (design and usage), networking and low-level communications libraries, consensus algorithms, and natural language processing. A bit of machine learning wouldn't hurt.

No one will be an expert in all (or even most) of these things, but if you've read "Designing Data-Intensive Applications" and already knew much of what was in there, we'd like to talk to you.

Unlike Silicon Valley, we do not discriminate based on age. Experience matters. We hire old people. (And young people, too.)

Principals only; no recruiters. Email cvsubmit /at/ relevantdb /dot/ com.
